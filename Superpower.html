Traits and power

The prototypes are the magician, the hero and the ruler.
Feora likes to fight and can feel a little impulsive sometimes. On the other hand, she can also be very calm and logical because she has contradictory personalities. She is very far-sighted and can predict things out of her mind. In order to achieve her goals, she can be extremly determined and sometimes even stuboborn.

She can only do short-term time leap now because she used too much of her power for the long-term time leap.
She can use mind read during daytime.
She has extremly strong leadership power that makes her be able to influence people with her willpower and makes people want to follow her.

Her only weakness is night. She tends to lose power at night but everything will back to normal once sunlight comes up.
